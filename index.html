Welcome to Beyond Rare. Please select your game:

Beyond Rare OG

This is the original version of Beyond Rare. You click a button and it gives you a rarity. The main function of the game is to test your luck. There is also a shop full of items and custom backgrounds.

Beyond Rare X

This version of Beyond Rare is a watered down version of the classic Beyond Rare. It still maintains the main functions of the classic Beyond Rare, but it is meant for old devices that may not be able to run the classic Beyond Rare.

Beyond Rare Y

Beyond Rare Y takes the classic experience and cranks it up! With expanded rarities, a bigger shop stocked with new items, and more customization options, this version lets you fine-tune your luck like never before.

Beyond Rare Z

Beyond Rare Z introduces a fresh take on the classic formula, developed by a different creator. With more rarities and unique mechanics, this version brings new challenges and unexpected twists to test your luck in exciting ways.
